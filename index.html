üé¥ Deck Building Rules

  • 3 Decks Required: Each player must bring exactly 3 different decks
  • 4-Card Difference: Each deck must have at least 4 different cards from the others
  • Energy Cards: Energy cards count toward the 4-card difference rule
  • Deck List: All decks must be submitted before the tournament starts

‚úÖ Deck Verification

  • Standard Verification: All player decks must be verified by Prajyot before the tournament begins
  • Special Case: Prajyot's decks will be verified by Vedant to ensure fairness
  • Verification Includes: Card count, 4-card difference rule, and deck legality
  • Deadline: All decks must be submitted and verified before Round 1 starts

üèÜ Match Format

  • Best of 3: Each match is best of 3 games
  • Different Decks: Must use a different deck for each game in a match
  • Winners Bracket: Win and advance, lose and drop to Losers Bracket
  • Losers Bracket: Win and continue, lose and you're eliminated
  • Grand Final: Winner of Winners Bracket vs Winner of Losers Bracket
